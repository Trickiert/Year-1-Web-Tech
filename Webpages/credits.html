Security Conciderations:


As this website is to be used by the general public there are a mumber of security rules and regulations that should be taken into conideration. For instance the protection of users personal details, which should be a main priority. Is is therefore advisable that encryption techniques be used to securely store users passwords and credit infomation in particular from hacking or phising attempts. This is an oversight by many orginizations who often use bad enctyption or even plain text files for passwords which are incredibly easy to be exploited if found by the wrong people. Other security threats such as webpage injecting, particularly for PHP and SQL should be taken into consideration and protection against them should also be prioritised to avoid faulty infomation being processed, database data loss or the spread of virsues through the website.

In addition to this there should be conciderations taken to make sure that user privicy is retained and that infomation is kept and used only when legally required or with the users consent. This is hugely important to avoid legal entanglements and potential felonies from being committed. This means that certain legaleslation such as the digital millenium act and the 1998 Data Protection Act must be enforced and followed as best as possible. The website should also make sure to inform the user of their terms and conditons and the legal precedents that they must follow too. Many websites can often be sued or held responsable for the actions of their customers and users therefore this is an important aspect to consider. However this tends to be more prominant for file sharing, video sharing or torrent websites it is still important to enforce this as to cover all legal bases and because it is good practice.
